Book a personal training session or group class in seconds.

Today's training centers are looking for ways to increase their clientele while keeping the quality of their services high. Let your clients book personal training or group classes online and make appointments with you in a breeze.

From your website

Let your clients visit your website and see personal training & group class schedule and make appointment. Easy Gym Software has a very flexible booking system which allows you to easily customize the look, feel and operation of your booking page. You can easily showcase personal trainers and group instructors on different pages of your website with their individual schedules, bios and photos.

From your QR Portal

QR BOOKING is the perfect logo & branding tool for your fitness studio and gym. Bring the convenience of making appointments right from your Facebook page or Instagram account. Create a branded QR standy for your reception and attract more customers.

By your front desk staff

With gym management software your front desk staff can set personal training & group class schedule and make appointment on behalf of your clients. They can also send reminder to clients and get updates about their sessions.
