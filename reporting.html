Reporting

Keep tabs on your metrics

Wherever you are, we've got your back. This app helps you run reports, bringing power and simplicity to the palm of your hand. We’re at the ready to help you run a successful business.

Instant access to the numbers that matter

If you want to grow your business, you need to know where it’s at. Our easy-to-use software gives you instant access to the numbers that matter—from revenue and retention to payroll and more. It tracks all aspects of your business so you can make smarter decisions.

Features

More Features

Sales
Know your revenue sources

Easy Gym Software has changed the way we do things. With our new RFID technology, clients can now mark their attendance in the gym with a band, card or keychain.

Payments
Keep tabs on your client sales

Keep tabs on your clients’ autopayment history. Maintain consistent revenue and budget confidently for the months ahead with a view into your clients’ autopayment history.

Clients
Gauge the success of your promotions

Our clients report is a great resource for gauging the success of your promotions and keeping track of how much revenue each client brings in. In addition to seeing which intro offers and deals bring in new business, you can view which services are most popular with your clients.

Inventory
Know when to reorder

Stay on top of your inventory with quick views of products in stock, being shipped or soon-to-expire. Know when to reorder and avoid running low. You can also see the profit margin by product or category.
