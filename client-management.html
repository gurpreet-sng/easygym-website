Workout
Plan

Workout Plan

Easy Gym software is the perfect workout planner for gyms and fitness studios. Create weekly workout plans with drag and drop template tools, send via email and SMS, update benefits and calories burn in workout plan, share videos related to specific plan.

Diet Plan

Diet Plan

Easy Gym software helps clubs and gyms to create quick diet plans and send them by email or SMS to their clients. Users can update the calories, proteins, proteins and hidden sugars in each food item. Also create private diet plans for each member.

Body Analysis

Body Analysis

Body analysis helps you evaluate how well your nutrition and fitness program are working. Record body measurements, BMI reports and skinfolds in your Gym Management Software.
