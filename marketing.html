Sell From Instagram

Easy Gym is the first software that integrates your Instagram account with an easy to use QR portal, creating a smooth and frictionless sale flow for your gym. It allows you to sell memberships, Personal training packages and any other type of package.

Retain clients with powerful, automated campaigns

CAMPAIGNS FOR GYMS:Easy Gym Software helps fitness studios retain clients and more importantly, increase revenue. Our automated campaigns help you send calendar reminders and discount offers to your client base.

SMS/Email Marketing

Easy Gym sends your members a series of automated messages, triggered by their membership or class history. We help you remind them to book their next gym session and keep them engaged with the benefits they’ve come to enjoy from your training facility.

Referral programs & campaigns

Our easy gym software is designed with your gym in mind. We know it’s important to offer your members the ability to reach out and connect with other like-minded people, so we make it easy with our interactive referral programs and campaigns.
